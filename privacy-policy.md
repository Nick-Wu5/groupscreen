# GroupScreen Privacy Policy

GroupScreen is a voluntary academic research tool developed at Purdue University.

**What we collect**
- Your first name, as entered during onboarding
- Aggregate screen time usage (total seconds per day) — no per-app breakdown
- Timestamps of key in-app events (e.g. when the group budget resets)

**Who can see it**
Your name and daily usage totals are visible to other members of your group only. No data is shared outside your group or used for any commercial purpose.

**What we don't collect**
- Which specific apps you used
- Location data
- Contacts or any other personal information

**Where data is stored**
Firebase Firestore, accessible only to members of your group enforced by server-side security rules.

**Study duration**
Data is collected for one active week. All identifiable data is deleted or anonymized at the conclusion of the study.

**Withdrawing**
You may withdraw at any time by tapping "Leave Study" in the app settings. This immediately removes all restrictions and stops all monitoring.
