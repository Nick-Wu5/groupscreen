# GroupScreen Privacy Policy

GroupScreen is a voluntary academic research tool developed at Purdue University studying the psychological effects of shared screen time accountability among peer groups.

**Researcher:** Nicholas Wu  
**Contact:** wu2264@purdue.edu  
**Institution:** Purdue University  

---

## What we collect

- Aggregate screen time usage (total seconds per day) — no per-app breakdown is ever transmitted off your device
- Timestamps of key in-app events (e.g. when the group budget resets, when the shared limit is reached)

## What we do not collect

- Which specific apps you used
- Location data
- Contacts, messages, or any other personal information
- Any data from devices other than your enrolled iPhone

## Who can see your data

Your first name and daily usage totals are visible only to other members of your group. Data is never shared outside your group and is never used for any commercial purpose. Access is enforced by server-side Firebase Firestore security rules — no member can access data from a group they do not belong to.

## Where data is stored

All data is stored in Firebase Firestore, a cloud database provided by Google. Data is accessible only to members of the same group, enforced by server-side security rules. Firebase's privacy policy is available at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

## How data is used

Data collected during the study is used solely for academic research purposes at Purdue University. Your identity will be anonymized in all research outputs, publications, and presentations. No identifiable data will ever be published.

## Study duration

Active data collection runs for one week. All identifiable data is deleted or anonymized at the conclusion of the study. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting wu2264@purdue.edu.

## Withdrawing from the study

You may withdraw at any time by tapping "Leave Study" in the app settings. This immediately removes all app restrictions, stops all monitoring, and unsubscribes your device from group notifications. Your data collected up to that point will be retained in anonymized form for research purposes unless you explicitly request deletion.
